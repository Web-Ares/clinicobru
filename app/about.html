About us

OUR Co-founders

Because patient recruitment is the number one cause of delay in clinical trials operations, the three university hospitals of Brussels have joined their efforts to set up the shared patient recruitment resources centre ClinicoBru.

By doing so they pursue a triple objective: improving patient access to clinical trials, supporting investigators in their recruitment efforts, achieving enrolment performances required by pharmaceutical companies. ClinicoBru vzw-asbl has been co-founded in 2014 with the help of Innoviris. the Brussels Institution for Research and Innovation.

Our Vision

The time of blockbusters is over. With the evolution towards personalized medicine, patient populations for clinical trials are becoming smaller and smaller. Pharmaceutical companies need to go for fewer study sites per country and more patients enrolled per site. To face these challenges and move to the next level of efficiency, recruitment should be based on pro-active tactics translated into continuous recruitment activities such as agenda based pre-screening, connecting with a pivot entity within the hospital or activating the right medical network.

These activities need to be designed based on the way patients access the healthcare system. They can involve other physicians in the same department, other departments in the same hospital and even other physicians in other hospitals and other healthcare professionals. To design, implement and follow up pro-active patient recruitment tactics, a new operational role called “Recruitment Facilitator” is needed to complete the pair Principal Investigator/Study Nurse.

OUR ADDED VALUE

Although patient recruitment is considered the number one cause of delay in clinical trial operations, this discipline is still struggling to be considered as a real expertise deserving its own budget line in the total amount allocated to a clinical trial.

Consequently, by lack of resources, PI’s are not able to implement really systematic, pro-active and continuous recruitment tactics. ClinicoBru provides an independent patient recruitment service based on 4 different packages. By becoming a “vendor”, ClinicoBru asbl-vzw allows Pharma’s to dedicate a budget to patient recruitment and makes it possible for PI’s to receive the necessary support to reach.

Florence Bosco Co-Founder and General Manager

Graduated from UMons as a Chemical Engineer in 2003, I first worked as a R&D Engineer at UCB and then as a Product Development Manager at Artelis, a start-up company now owned by Pall Corporation.

After these fascinating experiences, it was clear to me that the only thing who could make me professionally happy was to conduct innovation projects somehow related to Health, Science and Technology. In 2011, I was inspired by the TEDx talk of Peter Hinssen about the new normal of healthcare. He said: “We are the last generation that will know so little about our health.” When I got the proposal of taking the leadership of the ClinicoBru project back in 2013, I immediately thought that this was a unique opportunity to contribute to this big flip in healthcare.
