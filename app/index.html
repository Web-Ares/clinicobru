Clinical research is an essential mission for the progress of medicine. University hospitals and pharmaceutical companies carry it out, hand in hand.

Read more about us

Because patient recruitment is the number one cause of delay in clinical trial operations, the three university hospitals of Brussels have joined their efforts to set up the shared patient recruitment resources centre ClinicoBru. And when recruitment becomes faster, it means not only significant decrease in costs but it also enables earlier patient access to new treatments as well as earlier data publishing.

If you are a committed principal investigator or clinical operations professional from the pharmaceutical industry, our services are addressed to you. No matter if you are involved in phase I, II, III or IV trials or in trials involving medical devices, you might be enthusiastic to discover that we can provide operational support for the tricky task of patient recruitment.
