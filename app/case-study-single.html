Phase III study on Migraine prevention

Problem

Although migraine patients are mostly chronic patients, they usually don’t see their neurologist on a regular and predictable basis. They mostly come for a consultation when a crisis occurs. In this migraine study, the recruitment period was short and wouldn’t allow the PI to see all his patients. As he was willing to make sure all of them could get the information during the recruitment period and get the opportunity to participate, he decided to write them all a letter.

However, this would involve picking their mail address one by one in the EMR software of the hospital, which, for a list of around 1000 patients, means 3 full days of work. As in his team no one would have time to do that, he called ClinicoBru.

Outcome

Thanks to our well-established contractual framework with the hospital, our recruitment facilitator was able to make herself available for the job right after the site initiation visit. Consequently, the letters were sent at the very beginning of the recruitment period and within 3 months 10 patients where screened and 9 where enrolled via this channel.

Testimonials
